# MSV Consulting — Design Brainstorm

## Context
Bilingual (DE/EN) consulting website for MSV Consulting Vienna. Services: Grant Consulting, Startup Consulting, Investor Matching, AI Consulting. Target: Startups & SMEs. Owner: Maximilian Speiser-Villarroel. Brand colors: Blue + Green.

<idea>

**Design Movement:** Austrian Modernism meets Corporate Brutalism
**Core Principles:**
- Asymmetric, off-grid layouts with strong typographic hierarchy
- Raw structural honesty — borders, rules, and grids as visible design elements
- Confidence through restraint: very few colors, maximum impact
- Content-first: every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0B1F3A) as the dominant base — authoritative and trustworthy. Electric teal-green (#00C896) as the sole accent — forward-thinking and energetic. White for breathing room. The contrast is stark and intentional: this is not a soft advisory firm, it is a results-driven partner.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset-column layout — headlines bleed into the left margin, content columns are deliberately misaligned. Sections are separated by full-width horizontal rules rather than whitespace. The nav is a fixed left sidebar on desktop, collapsing to a top bar on mobile.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large outlined numerals (01, 02, 03) as section markers
- Thin horizontal rules with label text inline
- Bold uppercase section titles with letter-spacing

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactions are purposeful and immediate — no decorative animations. Hover states reveal underlines and color shifts. CTAs feel like physical buttons.

**Animation:** Entrance animations use fast, linear slide-ins (150ms). No bouncing or easing curves. Elements enter from the left margin.

**Typography System:** Display: "Syne" (extra-bold, geometric, Austrian-modern feel). Body: "DM Sans" (clean, neutral, readable). Hierarchy: 72px display → 48px H1 → 32px H2 → 18px body.

**Design Movement:** Contemporary European Corporate — Bold Geometry
**Core Principles:**
- Strong geometric shapes as structural and decorative elements
- High-contrast color blocking to create visual energy
- Split-screen and diagonal compositions to break monotony

**Color Philosophy:** Deep ocean blue (#0A2E52) as primary — depth and expertise. Vibrant emerald green (#10B981) as accent — growth and innovation. Near-white (#F8FAFC) for sections. Dark charcoal (#1A1A2E) for text. The blue-green pairing is energetic yet professional, perfectly suited for a modern Viennese consulting firm.

**Layout Paradigm:** Diagonal section dividers (clip-path) create visual momentum as the user scrolls. The hero uses a 60/40 split: left side has the headline and CTAs, right side has a bold visual. Stats section uses a dark background band to break the flow. Services are displayed in a 2×2 grid with hover-reveal descriptions.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section transitions (clip-path: polygon)
- Bold stat counters with green accent underlines
- Service cards with a left-border color accent that expands on hover

**Interaction Philosophy:** The site feels kinetic and forward-moving. Scroll-triggered animations reinforce the sense of progress. CTAs are prominent and action-oriented.

**Animation:** Scroll-triggered fade-up for content blocks (framer-motion). Counter animations for statistics. Diagonal clip-path transitions between sections create a sense of momentum.

**Typography System:** Display: "Outfit" (geometric, modern, confident). Body: "Nunito Sans" (friendly yet professional). Headlines are large and bold (700 weight), body text is comfortable at 16-17px.

## Selected Approach: **Contemporary European Corporate — Bold Geometry**

This approach best fits MSV Consulting because:
- The diagonal section transitions create a sense of forward momentum — ideal for a results-driven consulting firm
- The blue-green palette directly matches the client's brand color request
- The split-screen hero and geometric layout feel modern and premium without being cold
- The bold stat counters immediately communicate credibility (€4.5M raised, 60% success rate)
- The bilingual toggle fits naturally into the top nav
